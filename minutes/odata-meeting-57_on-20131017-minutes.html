5.1 Issues on CSD03 in New or Open state

Note:

Ralf received a comment, that the work products refer (non-normatively) to an IEEE floating point number specification, which is not available free for non-members of IEEE SA and that the wikipedia entries in this regard comparing german and english versions differ significantly, thus makng it possible, that non-interoperable implementations are being created. The proposal was made from the reporter, that we include text from the spec, but the members of the committee are reluctant to do so. Ralf will create a JIRA ticket to allow tracking this
