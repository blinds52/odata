5. OData Extension for Data Aggregation

5.1 Status of Public Review of OData Extension for Data Aggregation Committee Specification Draft 01

OData Extension for Data Aggregation Committee Specification Draft 01 successfully completed a 30-day public review on Sept 19, 2013. There were no comments arising from that public review. All issues filed so far on this spec have been addressed.

5.2 Pending issues?

6.1 Initial Proposal

What are the next steps?

  • All agree.
