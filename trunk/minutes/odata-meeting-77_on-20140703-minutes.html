5.1.5 ODATA-716 is OPEN

Mike summarizes that this issue is ready for resolution as well after Mike updates the proposal and Ralf has rolled back changes already made and not aligned with the proposed resolution as discussed during the meeting

Mike states the revised proposal: "Call out that If-Match only makes sense for operations on existing entities (update, delete). Doing a PUT/PATCH with no If-Match fails if the entity requires an ETag and already exists. Doing a PUT/PATCH with no If-Match can still be processed as an insert if the entity does not exist."

5.1.6 ODATA-631 is OPEN (noted in JIRA as applied)

Mike explains, that ODATA-700 clarifies the path syntax for the annotation element, ODATA-631 clarifies this for other elemnts (ie. non containment properties).

All discuss what parts shall be submitted to ISO and which will remain part of OASIS spec to keep eg. the vocabulary URLs stable and flexibly update the vocabularies in short cycles, as they are exactly designed to inject flexibility into the OData cosmos

All agree, that we will tackle ODATA-631 as part of OData v4.1
