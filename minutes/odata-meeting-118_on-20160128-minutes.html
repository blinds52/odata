7.2 ODATA-847 - Clarification: use of Schema namespace aliases within URLs

Mike: Proposal: Clarify that the aliases used in urls must be defined in the $metadata document of the service. Aliases used in referenced metadata documents are out of scope.

Ralf: Amended proposal: Clarify that the aliases used in urls must be defined in the $metadata document of the service. Aliases defined in referenced documents are out of scope.

Mike: I move we resolve ODATA-847 by clarifying that the aliases used in urls must be defined in the $metadata document of the service. Aliases defined in referenced metadata documents are out of scope. Ralf seconds.

Ram: No further discussion, no objection, motion passes unanimously.
